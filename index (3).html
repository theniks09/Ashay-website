About Us

Dedicated to Your Urological Health

Dr. Michael Harrison is a board-certified urologist with over two decades of experience in diagnosing and treating conditions affecting the urinary tract and male reproductive system. After completing his residency at Johns Hopkins Hospital, he has dedicated his career to providing exceptional patient care using the latest minimally invasive techniques.

Our Services

Comprehensive Urological Care

We offer a full range of urological services using the most advanced techniques and technology available.

Prostate Care

Comprehensive prostate screening, diagnosis, and treatment including BPH and prostate cancer care.

Robotic Surgery

State-of-the-art da Vinci robotic surgery for precise, minimally invasive procedures.

Kidney Stone Treatment

Advanced laser lithotripsy and shock wave therapy for effective kidney stone removal.

Men's Health

Erectile dysfunction, testosterone therapy, and complete male reproductive health services.

Urologic Oncology

Expert diagnosis and treatment of bladder, kidney, and testicular cancers.

Female Urology

Incontinence treatment, pelvic organ prolapse, and urinary tract conditions in women.
